Get to know each other better

I’m so happy you’re here. Grab a cookie, take a seat, and let me introduce myself. I’m Sally. I’m a mother, baker, blogger, author, photographer, and well-known lover of sprinkles. I’ve been in the kitchen for most of my life. From a very young age, I watched my mom and grandma work their baking magic, and once I picked up my first mixing spoon, I was hooked! I do most of my baking in Maryland with my husband (and number 1 taster), Kevin, and our beautiful daughter, Noelle. Of course, our family wouldn’t be complete without our two rescue dogs, Jude and Franklin. Baking has always been a welcome creative release for me (the warm cookies are a definite bonus!), and I’m blessed I can call it my career. When I’m not in the kitchen, I love running, hiking with my dogs, vacations at the lake, and experiencing all the exciting firsts with my baby girl. Life is good!
